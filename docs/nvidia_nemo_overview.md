# NVIDIA NeMo + Arize

NVIDIA NeMo is the NVIDIA-native framework for building, customizing, and deploying generative AI on NVIDIA GPUs and DGX systems, including NeMo Guardrails, NeMo Retriever, and NeMo Microservices. NeMo is the heart of NVIDIA's on-prem and sovereign-AI story.

Arize AI accelerates enterprise AI adoption on-premises with NVIDIA through the Arize AX + NVIDIA NeMo integration. The integration builds a data flywheel: production traces from NeMo-served agents are evaluated in Arize AX, and the resulting curated datasets are fed back into NeMo Customizer for retraining and Guardrails for policy tuning. This is the "self-improving AI" pattern.

Co-sell motion with NVIDIA:
- NVIDIA GTC main stage and Inception ecosystem events
- Joint deployment on NVIDIA DGX Cloud and on-prem NVIDIA AI Enterprise stacks
- Sovereign and regulated-industry deals where on-prem observability is mandatory

Typical Partner SA conversation: a regulated customer (finance, healthcare, government) deploys NeMo agents on-prem and cannot ship production traces to a third-party SaaS. Arize AX deploys inside the customer's NVIDIA AI Enterprise stack, providing the same observability and eval surface area without leaving the customer's perimeter.
